FAQ

Answers before we meet.

What service areas do you cover?

Stackly supports cloud migration, managed infrastructure, security, compliance, DevOps, data analytics, custom development, disaster recovery, consulting, and 24/7 technical support.

How does pricing work?

Managed service packages start with clear monthly tiers. Project pricing depends on scope, timeline, required specialists, and operational risk.

How long does implementation usually take?

Focused assessments can take 1 to 3 weeks. Migration, security, DevOps, and software programs usually range from 4 to 16 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you provide 24/7 support?

Yes. 24/7 support is available in Professional add-ons and Enterprise managed service agreements.

Can Stackly partner with our internal IT team?

Yes. Most engagements use a hybrid operating model where Stackly owns defined outcomes while internal teams retain business and system knowledge.

Do you support licensing and vendor contracts?

We support vendor evaluation, license planning, cloud marketplace guidance, and contract-ready technical scoping.

Can you handle regulated environments?

Yes. Stackly has experience with finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, SaaS, education, and enterprise compliance contexts.

What happens after the form is submitted?

You receive an on-screen confirmation, and our team reviews your request to schedule discovery, route support, or prepare a next-step proposal.
