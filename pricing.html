Pricing FAQ

Common questions before choosing a plan.

Can we switch plans later?

Yes. Plans can be upgraded or reduced at monthly renewal checkpoints after reviewing support volume, active risks, and committed work.

Do prices include cloud provider costs?

No. Cloud provider invoices, third-party licenses, and marketplace tools are billed separately unless a custom enterprise agreement includes them.

How does annual billing work?

Eligible fixed monthly plans receive a 20% discount when prepaid annually. Add-ons can be billed monthly or annually depending on scope.

Can we cancel?

Starter and Professional plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Enterprise contracts follow the service agreement terms.

Can we upgrade during an incident?

Critical escalation can be added immediately when specialist coverage is available. The commercial adjustment is confirmed after stabilization.
